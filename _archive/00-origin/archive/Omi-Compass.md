##  Agreement as Topological Augmentation

Agreement is the default augmentation operator of OMI.

When two or more intelligences reach a bounded relation, the topology changes.

Not because they merely “feel the same.”

Because they create a shared structure.

agreement
  ↓
boundary
  ↓
rule / fact / closure / combinator
  ↓
cons relation
  ↓
validated frame
  ↓
receipt
  ↓
topology update

This is why:

Agreement is all you need.

Agreement is not everything because everyone must agree.

Agreement is everything because any shared world requires a bridge.

No bridge, no topology.

No topology, no world.
